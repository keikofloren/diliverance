Loving Our Neighbours

What is Project Diliverance?

Project Diliverance is a youth-led mission trip to Dili, Timor Leste, in support of Cocoon's mission to change lives through education. Our trip included fun-filled activities such as games, dances, and skits, as well as the sharing of our personal testimonies, and small group prayer sessions, which culminated in a Christmas event for the children and youth.

Through Project Diliverance, we seek to connect with and show love to the local children and youth, for we were first loved by the Father and have been commanded to "love your neighbour as yourself" (Matthew 22:39).

  • Bringing Joy to the Village Children

    Together with Cocoon's Youth Leaders, we paid visits to the village children of Dili and Remexio, where we engaged them in fun outdoor games, song and dance.

  • Celebrating Christmas with the Youth & Kids Clubs

    We connected with the children and youth of Cocoon through lively games, colourful crafts, and skits of Bible stories. Our session with the Youth Club also involved a time of heartfelt worship, where we sang songs of praise in both English and Tetum, the local language.

  • Fellowshipping with the Cocoon Staff

    Our meals were dedicated to fellowship with the staff of Cocoon, where we learned more about each other's lives, cultures and experiences, and exchanged stories of our spiritual journeys.
