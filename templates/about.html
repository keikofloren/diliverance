About Dili

Dili - Capital of Timor Leste

  • • Dili is the capital of the youngest countries in the world, Timor-Leste.
  • • Houses half of the urban population (15% of Timor’s entire population).
  • • As of 2020, the adult literacy rate is 69.9% (below the world average of 86.81%).
  • • 42% of the Timor population lives below the national poverty line (one of the poorest countries in the world).
  • • Although Timor is predominantly Catholic (98%), many are Catholic by name only. They attend mass, but nonetheless still practice animal and nature worship.

  • Their Needs

    • • Education and employment prospects are elusive to the Timorese, rendering them stuck in a cycle of poverty.
    • • Flooding issues have left Cocoon with an urgent need of raising funds to flood-proof their school compound.
    • • This young and spiritually scarred country is in great need of prayer for a national transformation
